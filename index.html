Youssef Alami Mejjati


Hi there I am Youssef Alami Mejjati, a final year PhD student in the university of Bath, UK under the supervision of Dr Kwang In Kim and Prof Darren Cosker. My research is focused on computer vision and machine learning.
During my PhD I have been blessed to work on many projects in the fields of image editing, image synthesis (GANs and VAEs), Multi-Task Learning, image-to-image translation, Human attention redirection, Hand pose estimation.

Before the PhD I was studying in France, where I did my undergraduate in applied mathematics and statistics in Telecom SudParis (Institut Polytechnique de Paris), one of the leading engineering schools in France.

I am particularly interested in facilitating the image generation and editing pipelines for artists, professionals and regular users. Currently, advanced image creation requires professional software, human expertise and a lot of time. As a consequence, the creativity of many individuals cannot be seamlessly expressed. My goal is to solve this through AI powered algorithms allowing users to express their ideas and imagination in a controllable manner with only few clicks.

My hobbies include: Hiking, Travelling, Geography, Running, Climbing, Tennis.
