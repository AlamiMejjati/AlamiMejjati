Youssef Alami Mejjati


Hi there I am Youssef Alami Mejjati, a final year PhD student in the university of Bath, UK.
My research is focused on computer vision and machine learning. During my PhD I worked on many projects in the fields of
image editing, image synthesis (GANs and VAEs), Multi-Task Learning, image-to-image translation, Human attention redirection, Hand pose estimation. I am particularly interested in facilitating the image generation and editing pipelines for artists, professionals and regular users. Currently, advanced image creation requires professional software, human expertise and a lot of time. As a consequence, the creativity of many individuals cannot be expressed. My goal is to solve this through AI powered algorithms that allow users to express their ideas in a controllable manner with only few clicks.
